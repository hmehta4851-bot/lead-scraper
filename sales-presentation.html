1

City Selection — Smart Rotation

Each day the system picks a different Indian city in sequence — 8 Tier-1 cities first, then 20 Tier-2 cities. After all 28 cities are covered (28 days), it starts again. This ensures nationwide coverage without repeating the same city too often.

2

Lead Discovery — 3 Sources

For each of the 9 product categories, the system searches IndiaMART first, then JustDial, then Sulekha. It stops when it has enough quality leads for that product, avoiding wasted searches.

3

Quality Filtering — Only Complete Leads

A lead is only saved if it has ALL 5 required fields: Company Name, Contact Person, Phone Number, Email Address, and Designation. Incomplete leads are discarded automatically.

4

Deduplication — No Repeats

Before saving, the system checks whether the phone number already exists in the sheet. If it does, the lead is skipped. Your sheet never has duplicates.

5

Delivery — Sheet + Email

New rows are appended to your Google Sheet (one tab per product). An email summary is sent to hmehta4851@gmail.com showing how many leads were added per category today.

Technology

Technology Behind the System

The system is built entirely on free-tier infrastructure. There is no server, no monthly hosting bill, and no manual maintenance needed.

🐍 Python Script

Core scraping engine. Searches IndiaMART, JustDial, and Sulekha automatically. Visits company websites to fill in missing contact details.

⚡ GitHub Actions

Free cloud runner. Wakes up at 8 AM IST every day, runs the Python script, then goes back to sleep. No server, no VPS, no cost.

📊 Google Sheets

All leads stored in your Google Sheet. Share it with your entire sales team. Filter, sort, download — it's just a spreadsheet.

📧 Gmail Alerts

Daily email summary sent to hmehta4851@gmail.com showing exactly how many leads were added by city and product today.

🔄 Smart State Machine

Remembers which city was scraped last. Automatically rotates through all 28 cities before repeating. Never scrapes the same city two days in a row.

☁️ Google Cloud

Secure service account manages Google Sheets access. Uses GCP free tier — no billing for our usage volume.
